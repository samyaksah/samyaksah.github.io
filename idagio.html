CONTEXT

What is Idagio

Idagio is a classical music streaming service founded with the intent of creating a streaming experience tailored to the needs of classical music listeners. In doing so, they aim to establish themselves as the home for classical music, and in turn, secure the future of the genre with their platform. However Idagio currently expects its listeners to solely use their service as a means of discovering classical music and disregard their preferred existing method of discovery being online resourses like reddit and blogposts

Our solution brings Idagio to the listenersâ€™ preferred mode of classical music discovery (r/classicalmusic, talk-classical etc.) by providing tools that assist them to ease and centralize the music discovery experience. This was a 5 weeks long experience design project where-in we discovered a point of intervention to improve the classical music listening experience on Idagio.

PRODUCT BREAKDOWN

Instead of asking listeners to come to us, we should go to them.

The majority of its most active or vocal users are typically middle-aged, with sophisticated knowledge of classical music, though many new listeners will join these spaces seeking guidance. Through discussions members will commonly link each other music, and rely on this behavior as a method of content discovery and critique of classical music.

Idagio Embeds

If we intend for listeners to share IDAGIO links, we believe the embed is an important, first-impression touchpoint which should reflect the visual identity of the brand and its values clearly. Because of that, we've built the IDAGIO embed from the ground up to gear it specifically for classical music.

Highlights and Side-Bar

Recognized music is linked through IDAGIO's service, allowing listeners to mark pieces to revisit later, save pieces to personal collections, or search IDAGIO's entire library.

Link Menu

The Link Menu allows for seamless, rapid generation of hyperlinks to IDAGIO's music library. Listeners may find the music they wish to link immediately by typing its name, or locate music by narrowing results. Controlled entirely by keyboard interactions, the Link Menu brings IDAGIO's extensive library to a listener's fingertips.

Discovery Recap

The Discovery Recap presents listeners with an overview of all the music they have explored on various online resources through IDAGIO embeds, links, or extension-created interactions. By doing so, it gives listeners a reason to create an IDAGIO account and more comfortably make use of their service.

INITIAL FRAMING

Problem with IDAGIO

Idagio has identified the majority of their audience to include established classical music listeners, yet acknowledges a necessity to appeal to prospective listeners in order to achieve their goal of securing the future of the classical music genre. Through our own research, we have identified a notable quantity of inquiries regarding classical music, and reviewed studies indicating a prevalent willingness of prospective listeners to learn about the genre. Therefore, it would seem that Idagio's audience exists of both established classical music fans, as well as curious newcomers.

Taking into account this sliding scale of knowledge, we proposed an extension of their service designed for fluctuation, rather than extremes. Our method of doing so relies on the use of veteran knowledge and desire to engage in discussion as learning resources for newcomers through the use of a discussion board like platform.

Mockup of our inital framing focussed on afficianados helping newcommers using a discussion board-like framework


Problem with the Proposal

Based on the feedback we received, we acknowledged a necessity to differentiate from similar services such as Reddit, Medium, or SoundCloud to secure an audience. Initially, iterated on this proposal with the intention of finding what a substantial differentiator may be. However, we soon realized that there was a greater underlying problem: we were asking all participants and readers of classical music discussion to shift their venue to us, when many are existing comfortably where they are. Simply put, this demand was already being met.

FINAL FRAMING

Listeners tend find music online discussion platform

Our coming proposal to this problem, were guided by additional insight gathered by presenting these classical music communities with a survey. This survey reinforced that even listeners who believe their knowledge of classical music to be sophisticated desire to learn more, and that they do so from home through online resources, likely due to an indicated desire to discuss and share classical music.


Turning Point

With these new insights, we discovered that rather than try to own the discussion, asking listeners to migrate to IDAGIO, we should bring IDAGIO to the listeners in their own venues of discussion. By doing so, we intend to support an already-existing method of discovery, used by both newcomers and veterans, rather than force listeners to adapt to any IDAGIO-facilitated methods.


New Proposal

The idea behind our adapted proposal is to leverage established venues for discussion by providing flexible, discovery-supporting tools which reinforce IDAGIO as the home for classical music throughout various locations. These would take the form of a convenient embed designed for sharing classical music, and an extension which supports it. These discovery tools would then be reinforced through an in-app recap, intended to further support exploration and collection of music discovered through online rabbit-holing
