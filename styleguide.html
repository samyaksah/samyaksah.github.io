Paragraph

CSS selector:

No special classes

Sample code:

            <p>
            This was a course project that lasted for 2 months wherein we were tasked
            to learn about 3D Animation principles and apply them using Autodesk Maya
            to make a short film. My team and I were really interested in Mixed Reality
            and the possibilties that come with it, so, we decided to mix real life footage
            and 3D animation to make a story that tells a story of a average man with a dull
            life who get trasnported to a Virtual world and has to take part in a action
            packed race that with another car.
            </p>
